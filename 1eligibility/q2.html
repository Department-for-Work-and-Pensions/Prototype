Can you get Carer's Allowance?

Q2Do you spend 35 hours or more each week caring for the person you care for?

For example, cooking meals, or helping them with their shopping.

You can only qualify for Carer's Allowance if you are caring for this person , for 35 hours or more each week.

You are legally entitled to claim Carer's Allowance even though you do not qualify and will not get Carer's Allowance. If you wish to do so click the 'Next' button below.
