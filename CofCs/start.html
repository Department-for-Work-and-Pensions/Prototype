Carer's Allowance: report a change in circumstances

Not what you're looking for? ↓

You must report any change in your circumstances if you’re claiming or have applied for Carer’s Allowance.

This includes if you get a job, take a break from caring for someone or stop being a carer altogether.

What you need to know

Start now

  • Before you start
  • Other ways to apply

You must report when you:

  • take a holiday or a break from caring
  • change, start or leave your job
  • have to stay in hospital
  • stop caring for someone

You’ll need your National Insurance number and details of the person you’re caring for.

You can also report a change of circumstances to the Carer’s Allowance Unit by phone or by post.
